Suggested note types:

## Research Log

A lightweight log for what I’m reading or exploring.

- Why it matters
- Key takeaways
- Open questions

## Bookmarks & References

A simple evolving snapshot of current interests.

Possible sections:

- Thinking about: unresolved ideas and questions
